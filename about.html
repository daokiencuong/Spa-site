About

Brockville Regal Nails Salon & Spa is founded in 2024, committed to crafting stunning transformations with the power of eco-friendly products and sustainable practices in a clean environment at affordable prices.

“Our goal is to provide a place that each and every client who passes through the doors of our salon walks out feeling better than they did walk in.”
- Brockville Regal Nails Salon & Spa

Quality

At Brockville Regal Nail Salon & Spa, we took the time to find the best products in the industry for you. We carry…
  • DC gel polish & regular polish
  • CND shellac
  • OPI gel polish
  • Nugenesis Dip powders
  • Bio Gel
  • Solar Gel
  • Acrylic

Clean

In order to maintain a clean and safe environment, disposable files, buffers, slippers, gloves and toe separators are thrown out after one use.
Furthermore, we use PREempt (Accel) CS20 and PREempt RTU Disinfectant, a hospital grade sterilizer and high level disinfectant, as well as isopropyl alcohol for all pedicure tubs, shared surfaces, furnitures, and reusable tools after every client.
Payment
At Regal Nails & Spa Brockville, we accept: Debit, Visa, Mastercard and Cash for payment. However, tips are cash only!
