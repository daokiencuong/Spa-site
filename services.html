Nail Enhancements

*Dip Powders*

The dip powder system is strong, organic, odor-free and less damaging to the natural nails than the traditional acrylic nails - available in French, ombre ou colour.

*Bio Gel*

The dip powder system is strong, organic, odor-free and less damaging to the natural nails than the traditional acrylic nails or Bio gel is more flexible than the traditional acrylic or the dip powder system and a great alternative to strengthen your nails.

*Solar Gel*

Top-end brand for acrylic nail extensions that uses a specific type of powder and liquid to create a durable and long-lasting manicure. Usually last 4-6 weeks on average, but this can vary depending on aftercare and personal characteristics.

*Acrylic*

Full set nail extensions created by combining a liquid and a powder to form a paste that is sculpted over the natural nail and cured to create a hard, durable, and long-lasting manicure. Usually last 3-4 weeks on average, but this can vary depending on aftercare and personal characteristics.
